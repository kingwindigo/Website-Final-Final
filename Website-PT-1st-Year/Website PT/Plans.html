What to Bring for the Hike

Before heading out on your hike, make sure to pack the following essentials: comfortable hiking shoes, a water bottle, a first aid kit, snacks, sunscreen, a hat, and a map(Preferred) or GPS device. Depending on the weather, bring a lightweight jacket or raincoat. Remember to carry a backpack to hold everything securely. Don't forget to always inform members of the Green Explorer Club if you wish to proceed on your own or with a group! And lastly, Dont forget to take pictures of the spectacular sight, Enjoy your time, and Have fun!
